# The Zeta–Mass Closure Operator: Perfect Closure as an Eigenvalue Law

**John Van Geem / RQM Technologies**  
*Research Note — April 2026*

## Abstract

This paper proposes an operator-level synthesis: zeta closure and mass-shell closure are expressed as eigenvalue/kernel conditions of a shared closure formalism. We define a quaternionic dilation generator, its eigenmodes, and a zeta closure selector. We then present a dimensional lift into mass-shell form. The paper is programmatic and explicitly non-claiming with respect to proving the Riemann Hypothesis or deriving the Standard Model spectrum.

## 1. Thesis

A zeta zero is an internal Perfect Closure eigenvalue; a mass shell is a spacetime closure realization of such an eigenvalue under an additional scale map.

## 2. Quaternionic Dilation Closure Generator

Define
$$
\mathcal A_{\mathbf u}=\mathbf u\left(\rho\frac{d}{d\rho}+\frac12\right),\qquad \mathbf u^2=-1.
$$
Its eigenmodes are
$$
\chi_t(\rho)=\rho^{-1/2-\mathbf u t},
$$
with eigenvalue equation
$$
\mathcal A_{\mathbf u}\chi_t=t\chi_t.
$$
At prime scale $\rho=p$,
$$
\chi_t(p)=p^{-1/2-\mathbf u t}=\frac1{\sqrt p}e^{-\mathbf u t\log p}.
$$
So the critical-line prime phase appears as a dilation-eigenmode.

## 3. Zeta Closure Selector

Let
$$
\Xi(t)=\xi\!\left(\frac12+it\right),
$$
and define the functional-calculus selector
$$
\mathcal Z_{\mathbf u}=\Xi(\mathcal A_{\mathbf u}).
$$
If $t_n$ is a zeta-zero ordinate, then $\Xi(t_n)=0$, hence
$$
\mathcal Z_{\mathbf u}\chi_{t_n}=0.
$$
This is the zeta closure kernel condition.

## 4. Mass-Shell Lift (Formal Bridge)

Introduce a length scale $L_*$ and define a lifted invariant relation
$$
P\bar P=\left(\frac{\hbar t_n}{L_*}\right)^2.
$$
Then
$$
m_n=\frac{\hbar}{cL_*}|t_n|.
$$
This equation is a formal bridge unless $L_*$ is independently fixed.

## 5. What This Does and Does Not Claim

### Does not claim
- It does **not** claim a zeta zero is a particle.
- It does **not** prove RH.
- It does **not** derive physical masses without a justified $L_*$ mechanism.

### Does claim
- Both zeta closure and mass-shell closure can be expressed in eigenvalue/kernel language.
- A unified operator family is a coherent research direction.
