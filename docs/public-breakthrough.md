# The Public Breakthrough

## One sentence

## The intuition

Free light propagates: phase keeps moving forward without folding into a stable rest-frame pattern. Closed light is different: phase routes return and reinforce into a standing structure. Perfect Closure proposes that this standing, self-closed organization is what we recognize as massive matter. In short, light in open propagation is radiation; light in persistent closure is mass-bearing structure.

\(S^3 \times \mathbb{R}_s\), with shell number \(n\), shell lock \(s^2 = 2n\), spectrum
\(E_n = -\mathrm{Ry}/n^2\), and \(n^2\) degeneracy. That makes hydrogen a concrete calibration target for closure-shell geometry, not yet a completed theory of all atomic systems.
